About Me

Hello and welcome to my website, I am Lines Reflect! I come from the carved out caves of mountains that were shaped and chiseled by prehistoric waters. A place where only the strong survive. Where each day is a fight to keep one's lineage alive as it has been for millions of years. The day is just as dangerous as night. Sleep can put you in as much jeaopardy that being awake can. But that's what makes me strong. For that reason, any challenge I come across, I am grateful for. In fact, I seek danger, I run towards the flame, I code... Check out some of my projects below!
